I believe in using digital marketing to create a positive impact, whether that be for people or the planet.

What Job Opportunities Am I Looking For?

After graduating from the University of Virginia with a B.S. in Commerce in 2013, I joined the creative and analytical world of marketing.

I've been drawn to working at purpose-driven companies, from non-profits to eco-friendly companies to professional membership bodies. The one thing they all have in common is that I believe strongly in their mission and ability to make a positive difference.

I recently moved to New York in May and now I'm looking for digital marketing opportunities that will enable me to be creative and grow at a company that believes "doing good is good business".

WHAT HAVE I WORKED ON?

I've had the unique experience of working as a one-woman marketing team where I wore multiple hats, but I've also worked collaboratively across internals teams to successfully launch new products and events.

In addition, I've taken a UX Design course to create and test a new mobile app and helped a non-profit, The Plato Society, improve their paid search campaigns using a $10,000 monthly budget.

In my free time, I like to work on my travel blog and Instagram.
