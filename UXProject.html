In February 2019, I took part in Springboard's UX Design course. As part of the curriculum, I had to apply the concepts and skills that I learned in order to complete a capstone project that would take me through the entire UX design process from start to finish.


A mobile app to help shoppers better navigate grocery stores, saving them time and money

The Problem

Usually, grocery stores are large and hard to navigate, with aisles upon aisles of products to choose from. Grocery stores also often change their store layout. Some people want to save time or are in a rush to get in and get out. In addition, some people are looking to save money by sticking only to their grocery list or to stay healthy and not be tempted to buy junk food.
