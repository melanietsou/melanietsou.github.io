Springboard

As part of the curriculum for my Springboard Digital Marketing course, I ran a Facebook ad campaign to market one of Springboard’s free resources, an ebook titled “A Beginner’s Guide To Careers in UX Design”.

There were two main target markets for this campaign:

  • Employees in Related Industries who are looking to transition into the UX industry
  • Students in College/Grad School in cities with top Human-Computer Interaction Programs who are starting to look for their first job.
